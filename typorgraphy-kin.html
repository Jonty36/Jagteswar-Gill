Amsterdam KINETIC TYPOGRAPHY

Tasked with making an 8 second animation taking a word and transitioning it into a representation of visual representation of itself, I took the word Amsterdam and turned it into a horizon line of the Amsterdam canals and houses.

To begin the project making process, I started ideating about what word I wanted to use as the base of my Kinetic Typography Animation. Beginning with the words "Wake Up" I thought up of an idea to create a hand animated piece where it would tell a story of the words going through the process of waking up, however it felt too unprofessional so I took a step back and began ideating words again eventually falling upon the thought of making an animation for a city.

Role: Senior Designer, VFX Artist
